My name is Komali Kosaraju. I have just graduated out of 8th grade from Walsh Middle School. I’m excited to go into my freshman year at Round Rock High School. Some classes I’m eager to take next year are Pre-AP Computer Science, Choir, and Pre-AP Algebra 2. Some clubs I am thrilled to take next year are GirlUp and Debate. Some hobbies & classes I have outside of school are Tae Kwon Do, learning different coding languages, and participating in an online teen book club. Something I really enjoy is volunteering my time at the Round Rock Public Library, and at the Sai Baba Temple of Austin. Something that I really like about coding is that you can create new worlds with simple words , numbers, and punctuation. I wanted to participate in the CodeChicas program because I thought that it would be fun and educational. One goal that I have set for myself in the future is to go to UT Austin with a major in Computer Science and Engineering.
